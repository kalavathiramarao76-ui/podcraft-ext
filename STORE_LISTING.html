Detailed Description

EpicClip is an AI-powered podcast repurposer that transforms any podcast episode into 6 different content formats. Stop spending hours manually creating social posts, newsletters, and show notes from your episodes.


Summarize: Extract key points, main arguments, and actionable takeaways from any episode.


Clips: Identify the most shareable segments with timestamps, ready for short-form video or audiograms.


LinkedIn Posts: Generate engagement-optimized LinkedIn posts with hooks and CTAs from episode insights.


Twitter Threads: Break down episodes into tweet-sized insights, each under 280 characters, threaded for reach.


Newsletter Edition: Complete newsletter with subject line, intro, key sections, and call-to-action.


Show Notes: Structured show notes with timestamps, chapter markers, guest bios, and resource links.


Generate All: Batch mode creates all 6 formats at once. Works directly on YouTube and Spotify with injected buttons.
